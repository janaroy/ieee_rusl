IEEE INSL Awareness session - RUSL edition 2019

By: Sobia


IEEE Innovation nation Sri Lanka had their last INSL Awareness Session at the Faculty of Applied Sciences at the Rajarata University of Sri Lanka on 10th of July. It mainly targeted the students of First two years. Who are following Information and Technology and related degrees but there were students participating who did not essentially belong in this category. This proved that people still have interest from other faculties as well. The first session of the event happened to be a comprehensive yet very engaging introduction to IEEE and what it is standard for . The session was conducted by Gobiga Rajalingam, Assistant Secretary of IEEE student branch in Rajarata University of Sri Lanka.It was explained what is IEEE membership and their key competition like INSL, Xtream and etc. It was clarified about IEEE and made the audience to know everything about IEEE.

And the next session was on what is INSL and how it organised by Maheshika Madubashini secretary of Innovation Nation Srilanka 2019. She discussed about the INSL Competition and the rules and regulation of that competition.

Next program was game Session. They asked some questions about the things relates to IEEE and INSL. Those who answered the question were gifted.

The last session was ideation session which induced our innovations where participants told our ideas regarding solutions for problems in university domain. Best ideas were gifted and event ended successfully.
