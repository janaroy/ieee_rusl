IEEE SLSYWC 2018-Augmenting into a Digital Dimension

By: Aarani Mahaganapathi


IEEE SLSYWC 2018 was held from 28th December 2018 to 30th December 2018.It is the very first congress for our Student Branch.Nine of us went from our student branch.We were boarded in Sri Lanka Technological Campusâ€™ hostel for 3 days.Congress Opening ceremony and IEEE Sanniya day was the first day which is Friday 28th of December.Registrations took place and students were admitted to the hostels and assigned to groups with breakfast,lunch and dinner tokens at around 9AM onwards.We were welcomed with welcome drink.

Then at around 1030AM we had an ice breaking ,networking session as group wise which was conducted by punishment team.After that we took our lunch.Then we had been directed to the relevant halls.

After that opening ceremony began with video of the congress,lightening oil lamps and welcoming guests.Then SLTC vice president for academic affairs Prof.Ashok Polpitiya addressed the gathering.

Then Dr.Tomy Sebastian made his keynote on Advances in Automotive Electrification.It took about nearly 40 minutes.After that there is a plenary session which diverted our minds and made ourselves refreshing.We are taught with dance steps we had to perform at the closing ceremony.

After that we had a great time with penalty team.They had told some rules we have to follow at congress and they said they are very strict and will punish those who are breaking rules.It is somewhat funny listening to their conversations.They made awareness about mobile event app Echowave we used for congress.

Then there is an interactive session by Kavindya Tennakon about creative confidence.She is very much interactive and she got our ideas regarding creativity too.She announced that if we have idea regarding startups she will arrange funding for 3 ideas.It seemed as a great opportunity to those participants.

Then there again a session regarding putting technology in service of primary industry by an Australian example conducted by Prof.Yousef Ibrahim.

After that we had a group activity during tea time.It is indeed a good activity we counted the stairs,took group selfie in front of main satellite and so on.

We had a session called IEEE for dummies where Subodha Charles and Pasan Pathiyagoda talked about how they got into IEEE and now what they gained from IEEE.This talk is mainly focused on anyone can volunteer for IEEE even if they are not so technical and through their efforts ,they can get something meaningful from it.

Then we again had a plenary session and we were asked to go to the hostel and get prepared for IEEE sanniya.We went to hostels and got ready with our traditional dresses.

Highlights of the first day was indeed IEEE sanniya.It was held in SLTC premise,the decorations were really beautiful and traditional.We enjoyed sitting outside at night with beautifully worn traditional dresses.The whole session were very funny and very much interacted with the audience.

Student branches those applied performed events individually and group wise.The events were singing,dancing and dramas like that.Then finally they announced the winner by considering team effort.We were provided with snacks during Sanniya.The day was completed with so many beautiful moments.

The second day was filled with outbound sessions,Awards nights and so on.The day 29th December 2018 was started around 7AM for us with breakfast.Early beds who came to dining area early were gifted.Congress dance steps were taught again in Morning plenary session.

Then we had an introductory session on IEEE Industry Applications society by Dr.Tomy Sebastian.It was very useful for us and he gave free 1 year membership for IAS for those willing.We had it for 30 minutes.

After that was the most awaited technical session-Success story of a Sri Lankan Entrepreneur.PickMe founder Zulfer Jiffrey did the session regarding his success.It is indeed a great opportunity to those who are willing to gain success in the industry and it was a motivation to those who has better ideas.

Next session also somehow related to PickMe.PickMe CTO Mithila Somasiri spoke about their platform and technology.So from that we got to know what technologies used in PickMe.

Then we enjoyed morning tea,parallel to IEEE innovation nation Sri Lanka Finale ,we had outbound sessions as teams.There we played a lot of refreshing games which we only watched in TV reality shows.We enjoyed playing as a group and playing those wonderful games.

After that we had lunch.Then there was a panel discussion by Mr.Rohan Jayaweera,Mr.channa De silva and Mr.Sanjeewa sunaweera.It is about what industry is expecting from a fresh graduate.We gained knowledge regarding what it actually expects.They stressed our soft skills.

Then we took lots of group photos.Then we had plenary session and after that there are talks by IEEE Young Professionals.

After evening tea,we got ready for IEEE awards night in formal dresses.Awards Ceremony was commenced with welcoming guests and lightening oil lamps.Awards ceremony was colorful with photoshoots and happiness.First,there is a cultural performance by SLTC students which was really eye catching.Speeches in the awards ceremony also seemed to perfect and shortened.Awards were given to the most successful initiatives by student branches,Young Professionals and Women In Engineering.When we saw them awarded ,we were very enthusiastic about doing something productive.

Then we had Gala dinner.It was very tasty with deserts.After that we danced with DJ at party.It is a great experience for us and we enjoyed a lot.This day also completed with evergreen memories.

The last day of IEEE SLSYWC 2018 ,Sunday 30th of December also started around 7AM.Eary birds were gifted on that day too.We had a morning plenary session where we were taught congress dance again.After that we had an introductory session on IEEE Power and Energy Society by Prof J.P.Karunadasa.Then we had another introductory session on IEEE Industrial Electronics Society.

After having Tea,there were three sessions went simultaneously.One is Student Track by Prashanth Mohan introducing IEEE MadC and Xtreme.Other one is Young Professionals track by Mr.Kavinga Yapa Abeywardana regarding essentials of cyber security and introduction of Young Professionals activities.

WIE track is mainly AGM of WIE and there was also a panel discussion.Then we had our lunch.

After that we had a session called Sri Lanka after independence which was conducted by Mr.Eranda Ginige.Then We had a session about Humanitarian issues by Mr.Sulaiman Rameez.It is very beneficial because IEEE is mostly about advancing Humanity.Then we had our last session product design engineering for the development of Sri Lanka.We learned so many new things from this session.

After that we had our last plenary session which was filled with our feedbacks.Then there was a prize giving for the winners of outbound sessions and other competitions.Our Student Branch got the prize for video contest.It was an amazing experience for us.Then we performed that congress dance as farewell dance.Congress ended with vote of thanks officially on 30th December 2018.
