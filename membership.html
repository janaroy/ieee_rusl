IEEE Student Branch of Rajarata University of Sri Lanka

In 2018, IEEE Student Branch of Rajarata University of Sri Lanka Student Branch started with around 19 student members under the guidance of our counselors and the Founding Chair Mr.Isuru Supasan. Through challenges and obstacles that accompany a new initiative, we are going strong with the support of the IEEE Student members and the volunteers of RUSL. Bringing about a pool of active IEEE members who have spread their wings out in the IEEE Sri Lanka Section via being IEEE SL SYW Congress 2019 Secretary, IEEE Innovation Nation Sri Lanka 2019 Secretary, Design Lead, IEEE SL Inspi, IEEE TechnoMeetup and more has thus proven that our Student Branch has made quite an impact in the minds of the youth within a short period of time since the founding of the Branch.
