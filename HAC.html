Humanitarian Activities Awarness Session

By: Sobia

What is IEEE HAC? This was a question for a majority of the students here at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. IEEE Humanitarian Activities Awareness Day, organized by the IEEE Student Branch of RUSL, was able to provide an indeed good answer for this question.

This awareness session was successfully held on 6th of February 2019, at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, from 4.00 PM onwards. Both IEEE members and non-IEEE members, from the Faculty of Applied Sciences, Faculty of Technology and Faculty of Management Studies, participated in this event. The basic idea of the IEEE Humanitarian Activities Awareness Day was to enlighten the student community about what Humanitarian is, along with a brief introduction about the IEEE Humanitarian Activity Committee. The session commenced with the introductory presentation, which was done by Gobiga Rajalingam, the Assistant Secretary of the IEEE Student Branch of Rajarata University Sri Lanka. The main lecture of this session, which was lined up next, was done by Dr. Wasantha Sena Waliange.

His mind blowing speech gave the audience a clear idea about what humanitarian activities are, and the importance of humanitarian activities. Furthermore, he also made the audience identify the real humanitarian issues that needed to be observed and sought solutions for. Dr. Wasantha Sena Waliange had an amazing live musical event included in his awareness session. That instrumental performance indeed took the audience to another level, and of course, it added more glamour to the IEEE Humanitarian Activities Awareness Day of RUSL.

After that informative, yet interesting session conducted by Dr. Wasantha Sena Waliange, IEEE Humanitarian Activities Awareness Day concluded with the Vote of Thanks, done by Pavithra Nanayakkara, the Secretary of the IEEE Student Branch of RUSL. Towards the end of this event, the students who were gathered, were certainly motivated to take a step forward and participate in humanitarian activities. This marked yet another remarkable event of the calendar of the IEEE Student Branch of Rajarata University Sri Lanka.
